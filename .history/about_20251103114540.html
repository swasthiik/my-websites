Dr. Dipali Premalkumar Patel

With over 15 years of dedicated experience, Dr. Dipali is a certified Marma, Magnetic & Sujok Therapist committed to promoting holistic well-being through natural healing techniques.

Our History & Journey

  • 2007: Completed M.D. from Indian Academy of Acupressure Science, Indore University, Sri Lanka.
  • 2013: Acquired Doctor's Degree in Naturopathy of Science.
  • 2016: Completed Dietician Courses from JSRFC Trust.

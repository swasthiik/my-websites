About Dr. Dipali Patel

Dr. Dipali Patel, founder of Harpal Acupressure, is a certified holistic therapist with years of experience in alternative medicine and energy healing. Her mission is to guide individuals toward complete well-being using natural, non-invasive therapies such as Acupressure, Sujok, Reiki, and Vastu Consultation.

Under her guidance, hundreds of clients have experienced lasting relief from stress, pain, and imbalance through customized healing programs. Her approach integrates scientific precision with compassionate care, helping every individual achieve balance in body, mind, and soul.

Her clinic promotes awareness of natural healing practices and regularly conducts training programs, wellness workshops, and certification courses to spread holistic health education.

About Our Practice

Harpal Acupressure is a trusted wellness center dedicated to natural healing through acupressure and holistic therapies. We believe that true healing begins from within when energy balance and harmony are restored.

Led by Dr. Dipali Premalkumar Patel, a certified Marma, Magnetic & Sujok Therapist, our center blends traditional healing with modern wellness techniques.

With over a decade of experience, Dr. Dipali has helped numerous people overcome pain, fatigue, and emotional stress through natural, non-invasive healing methods.

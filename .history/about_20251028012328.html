About Harpal Acupressure

Journey & Achievements

  • 2005 – Completed Basic Acupressure Course from Indira Gandhi Open University (IGNOU).
  • 2007 – Completed M.D. from Indian Academy of Acupressure Science, Indore University, Sri Lanka.
  • 2007 – Gold Medal recognition by Charotar Moti Satyavis.
  • 2009 – Gold Medal from Indian Academy of Acupressure at 8th National Acupressure Conference.
  • 2010 – Became a certified member of Rajasthan Vaikalpik Swasthya Sanstha (Acupressure Board).
  • 2013 – Awarded Doctor’s Degree in Naturopathy Science.
  • Organised and managed multiple 3-day camps on Acupressure & Sujok Therapy (Nasokvala Hall Ode Bazar, Umreth).
  • Held 3-day camp in Nasik for Acupressure & Sujok Therapy organised by Jalaram Temple Trust and Maheshbhai Shah.
  • 1-day camp in Jubilee Baug, Vadodara for Sai Baba Temple Trust.
  • Debate programme on Acupressure broadcasted on “AKASHVANI” (Vadodara) – 25 Dec 2010.
  • Regular articles published in health magazine “Arogyamantra”.
  • 2010 & 2012 – Delivered lectures for senior citizens in Anand (organised by Lion Club).
  • Held 15–20 lectures and training camps in Vadodara city for senior citizens and wellness seekers.
  • Delivered lectures in Pune at “Shivyog” events (Science Beyond Science).
  • Conducted training for GER GGEB Company, Vadodara.
  • 2016 – Completed Dietitian Course from JSRFC Trust (16 Dec 2016).
  • 6 Aug 2017 – Gold Medal at National Conference on Health & Wellness (Heritage Foundation).
  • 16 Feb 2018 – Honoured by Association of Alliance Clubs International (District 123, Ahmedabad).
  • Received “Shrimani Award” at National Conference on Health & Wellness through Holistic Approach (2017, Vadodara).
  • Received “Diamond Award” from Acupressure Training & Research Centre (2018, Lokmat Media Partner).

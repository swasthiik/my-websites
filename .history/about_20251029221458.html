About Harpal Acupressure

Harpal Acupressure was founded with a vision to bring holistic healing and natural balance to people’s lives. Under the expert guidance of Dr. Dipali Premalkumar Patel, we combine age-old acupressure wisdom with modern wellness practices. Our treatments aim to restore energy flow, relieve chronic pain, and promote mental, physical, and emotional harmony.

With a compassionate approach and years of expertise, Dr. Dipali has helped numerous patients experience the power of alternative healing. She specializes in Acupressure, Sujok Therapy, Magnetic Healing, Reiki, and Marma Therapy.

At our clinic, we believe that true wellness is achieved through balance — not just physical but also mental and spiritual well-being. Our space offers a calming environment where healing happens naturally.
